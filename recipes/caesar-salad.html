Caesar Salad

Home

Caesar salad is an absolute classic, nothing beats the a great caesar with a creamy delicious sauce and a beautiful crunch from fresh romaine lettuce and tasty crutons

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely choppewd
  • 1⁄2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 dash worcestershire sauce
  • 1⁄4 olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Salad

  • 1⁄2 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1⁄4 grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoonds croutons
  • (Optional) 100 grams bacon, cooked, diced
  • (Optional) 1 egg, boiled, sliced in half

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. To make the dressing: Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl.
    Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce; whisk until smooth and creamy.
    Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Make salad: Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated.
  4. Top salad with croutons and egg.
  5. Serve.
  6. Enjoy!
